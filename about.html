ABOUT WEAR KAPAMPANGAN

SULUD ME

The germinal concept started upon the realization that most of the new generation of youth in Pampanga no longer want to speak Kapampangan, as they have low esteem for the language. They prefer the Tagalog imposed by the national government. The goal and intention of the statement T-shirts is to instill in the minds of these new generations that Kapampangan is cool.

We felt that T-shirts were the best medium for our advocacyâ€”T-shirts being everyday utilitarian objects. We thought of doing T-shirts because everybody wears T-shirts. We started selling them to our office mates. And the feedback was good.

For Us, Kapampangan is not merely a language, but a concept embedded in the culture and sensibilities of the one who speaks it. You can see that even within Kapampangan region, proverbial verses are spoken from barrio to barrio. Each has its own identifiable statements.

The first designs we created were typographic. "I remember going to Manila and buying ordinary polyester T-shirts where we first printed the Kapampangan statement. We first did: 'Atin maglakad kanu keng kalye Balibago, misakab ya ing alti! Sabi na, TAKSYAPOW! [An American was was walking along the street in Balibago when he suddenly slipped and said, TAKSYAPOW!]' All the 30 T-shirts that we printed bearing the statement were sold out.
